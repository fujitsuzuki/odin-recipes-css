Broccoli slaw

Description

This broccoli slaw recipe was passed around for years in my grandmother's beauty salon. She took it to every church supper. It was on the table every Thanksgiving and Christmas.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 packages oriental-flavour ramen noodle soup
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1 package broccoli coleslaw mix
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion

Dressing

  • 1/2 cup salad oil
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 Celcius degrees
  2. Melt butter, crush ramen, stir crushed noodles, almonds and sesame seeds into melted butter. Spread mixture on a baking sheet
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and crunchy, 8-10 minutes; allow to cool completely
  4. Toss together coleslaw mix and green onion in a large serving bowl
  5. Make dressing: whisk together iol, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce and reserved seasoning pakcets in a separate bowl until well combined
  6. Stir cooled noodle mixture into dressing. Pour dressing over coleslaw-onion mixture; toss to coat
