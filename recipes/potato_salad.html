Potato salad

Description

This Southern potato salad includes eggs, celery, and relish and should be served warm.

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 stalk celery
  • 1/4 cup sweet relish
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook potatos until tender but still firm
  3. Drain and chop potatoes
  4. Boil egss, peel and chop
  5. Combine potatoes and eggs with mayonnaise, celery, relish, garlic, mustard, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  6. Gently mix together and serve
